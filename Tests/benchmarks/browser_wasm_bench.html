Reading the numbers. Each row runs both paths on N deterministically-generated axiom keys, R trials each (warm-up trial discarded). The reported ms is the median of the R trial totals. Per-op is median ÷ N × 1000 in microseconds. State agree confirms the two paths produced the identical lcm(p₁, …, p_N) BigInt — correctness gate, not speed. A row with agree: false is a cross-runtime bug, not a benchmark result; surface it rather than interpret speeds.
