Mission Statement

                    
SUM is a knowledge distillation platform that harnesses the power of AI, NLP, and ML to extract, analyze, and present insights from vast datasets in a structured, concise, and engaging manner. The goal is to summarize content into a succinct & dense human-readable form allowing one to "download" knowledge quickly.
